## Hunter vs Maker

**Option A: Self-Hunt** (No hunter)
- Pros: Keep control, authentic story
- Cons: Less initial boost

**Option B: Get a Hunter**
- Pros: Hunter's audience upvotes early (momentum)
- Cons: Sharing spotlight, need to find someone

**Recommendation for this project:** Self-hunt
- Personal story is the main hook
- Authentic solo founder narrative
- No need for external validation

If getting a hunter:
- Target: Someone in FIRE/personal finance space
- Reach out 1 week before launch
- Offer: Early access, thank them publicly

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

‚ùå Launching without preparing supporters (need 20-30 people ready to upvote in first hour)  
‚ùå Ignoring comments (reply to EVERY comment within 1 hour)  
‚ùå Not being online all day (launch day = 12+ hours active)  
‚ùå Generic first comment (tell a personal story, not a sales pitch)  
‚ùå Bad screenshots (blurry, cluttered, or too much text)  
‚ùå Launching on Monday (weekends have better engagement)  
‚ùå No social proof (get beta testers BEFORE launch)
