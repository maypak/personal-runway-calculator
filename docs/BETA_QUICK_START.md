## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### Tip 1: Be Realistic with Expenses
Don't underestimate! Include:
- Yearly expenses (divide by 12): insurance, car registration, gifts, travel
- "Surprise" expenses: medical, home repairs, etc.
- Buffer: Add 10-20% for unexpected costs

### Tip 2: Track Variable Income
If your income fluctuates (freelancer, contractor):
- Use **average monthly income** (last 3-6 months)
- OR use **minimum monthly income** (conservative estimate)

### Tip 3: Update Monthly
Your runway changes as you:
- Spend money (savings decrease)
- Earn money (savings increase)
- Change jobs (income changes)

Set a reminder to update your numbers monthly!
