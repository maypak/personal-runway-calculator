- Storytelling (personal journey)
- Vulnerability (share struggles)
- Helpfulness (tips, not just promotion)
- Community (ask questions, listen)

## Growth Tactics

### Pre-Launch
1. **Build relationships** - DM 20 relevant accounts
2. **Join conversations** - Reply to 10+ tweets/day
3. **Value-first** - Share tips, not just self-promotion
4. **Beta social proof** - Share tester feedback

### Launch
1. **Tweetstorm** - Launch announcement thread (8-12 tweets)
2. **Tag supporters** - Thank beta testers publicly
3. **Product Hunt** - Cross-promote PH launch
4. **Engage all day** - Reply to every mention

### Post-Launch
1. **User success stories** - Feature users' runway calculations
2. **Weekly updates** - What we shipped, what's next
3. **Ask questions** - "What feature do you want next?"
4. **Be consistent** - 1-2 tweets/day minimum
