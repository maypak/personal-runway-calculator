## Lessons from Successful Launches

**What works:**
‚úì Personal story (authentic, vulnerable)  
‚úì Clear value prop (solve one problem well)  
‚úì Fast response times (treat PH like AMA)  
‚úì External traffic (Twitter, Reddit, email)  
‚úì Visual polish (screenshots, demo video)  
‚úì Genuine engagement (not robotic)

**What doesn't work:**

‚ùå Fake scarcity ("Only 24 hours!")  
‚ùå Ignoring criticism  
‚ùå Copy-paste replies  
‚ùå Disappearing after launch  
‚ùå Over-promising features

## Emergency Playbook

**If ranking is low at Hour 6:**
- Don't panic (rankings fluctuate)
- Double down on engagement
- Drive external traffic harder
- Ask supporters to comment (not just upvote)
- Focus on quality discussions

**If getting negative feedback:**
- Don't argue
- Acknowledge valid points
- Fix bugs ASAP
- Thank them for honesty
- Show roadmap (we're listening)

**If something breaks:**
- Announce it immediately
- "We're aware of [bug], fixing now"
- Post update when fixed
- Apologize, move on

**If you're exhausted:**
- Take a 30-min break
- Have coffee ready
- Remember: it's a marathon, not sprint
- Delegate comment replies (if you have a co-founder)
