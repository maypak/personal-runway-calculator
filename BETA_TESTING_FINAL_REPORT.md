## ğŸ“ Lessons for Next Product

### What Worked
1. **AI Beta Testing** - Saved 2-3 weeks of human coordination
2. **Persona Diversity** - 5 segments revealed different pain points
3. **Design-First** - UI investment paid off immediately
4. **Feature Uniqueness** - Income tracking = differentiator

### What We'd Do Differently
1. **Earlier i18n** - Should've launched bilingual from day 1
2. **Scenario Comparison** - This should've been MVP feature
3. **Segment Validation** - Should've picked 1-2 segments to start
4. **Pricing Research** - Could've asked pricing questions earlier

### Process Improvements
1. **Add payment validation** - Ask "Would you pay now?" not just "Would you pay?"
2. **Shorter tests** - 30min too long, aim for 15min
3. **More technical testing** - Need edge cases, not just UX
4. **Competitor comparison** - Ask "vs [competitor], why this?"
