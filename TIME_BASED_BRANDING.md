## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Do âœ…

- Frame money as TIME
- Use empowering language ("freedom," "opportunity")
- Focus on what users CAN do (not what they can't)
- Make time tangible (days, hours, not just months)
- Celebrate small wins ("You just saved 3 days!")

### Don't âŒ

- Compare to dystopian movies (no "In Time" references)

## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Too Preachy

**Concern:** "Every dollar = time" might feel guilt-trippy

**Mitigation:**
- Balance: Awareness, not judgment
- Tone: Empowering, not shaming
- User control: They decide what's worth it

---

### Risk 2: Overcomplication

**Concern:** Adding more context (days/hours) = clutter

**Mitigation:**
- Progressive disclosure (show months by default, expand to days)
- Keep it optional (toggle advanced view)
- Test with users first

---

### Risk 3: Cultural Sensitivity

**Concern:** "Time is money" may not resonate globally

**Mitigation:**
- A/B test in different markets
- Allow users to choose framing (money vs time view)
- Localize messaging
